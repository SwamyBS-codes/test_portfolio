Case study

How a cluttered homepage became a cleaner, faster, and more persuasive experience.

This page is useful if you want to show process as well as output. It explains the problem, the approach, and the result in a simple format.

Outcome

Clearer messaging, improved mobile hierarchy, and a stronger call to action.

Problem

The original page tried to do too much at once. Visitors had to work to understand the offer, find proof, and decide what to do next.

Approach

Clarify the messageMake the value proposition visible in the first screen.
Reduce frictionSimplify navigation and remove distracting sections.
Improve proofSurface testimonials, outcomes, and examples earlier.

Result

+28%higher inquiry rate
40%less scrolling to contact
2xfaster content comprehension
